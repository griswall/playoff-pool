Pool Rules

Welcome to the Hurd NFL Playoff Pool! Here's how to play:

1. Drafting Your Team:

  • You have a salary cap of $38.5M to build your team.
  • Your roster must consist of 10 players:
    • 1 Quarterback (QB)
    • 3 Running Backs (RB)
    • 3 Wide Receivers (WR)
    • 1 Flex (RB/WR/TE)
    • 1 Defense/Special Teams (D/ST)
    • 1 Kicker (K)
  • You can select a maximum of 3 players from any single NFL team.

2. Scoring:

(Example Scoring - This needs to be defined by you!)

  • Passing TD: 4 points
  • Passing Yards: 1 point per 25 yards
  • Rushing/Receiving TD: 6 points
  • Rushing/Receiving Yards: 1 point per 10 yards
  • Reception (PPR): 1 point
  • Interception Thrown: -2 points
  • Fumble Lost: -2 points
  • Kicker FG Made (0-39 yds): 3 points
  • Kicker FG Made (40-49 yds): 4 points
  • Kicker FG Made (50+ yds): 5 points
  • Kicker PAT Made: 1 point
  • D/ST TD: 6 points
  • D/ST Sack: 1 point
  • D/ST Interception/Fumble Recovery: 2 points
  • D/ST Points Allowed (0): 10 points
  • D/ST Points Allowed (1-6): 7 points
